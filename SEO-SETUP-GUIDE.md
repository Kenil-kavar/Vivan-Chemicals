## üöÄ What You MUST Do Next

### 1. Update URLs in These Files (CRITICAL!)

Replace `https://yourdomain.com` with your actual domain in:

- **index.html** (lines 13, 16-19, 25-29, 40)
- **public/sitemap.xml** (all URLs)

### 2. Submit to Google Search Console

**This is the MOST IMPORTANT step!**

1. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Add your property (website)
3. Verify ownership (easiest: HTML file upload or DNS verification)
4. Submit your sitemap: `https://yourdomain.com/sitemap.xml`
5. Use "URL Inspection" tool to request indexing of key pages

**Without this step, Google won't know your site exists!**

### 3. Set Up Google Analytics (Recommended)

1. Create account at https://analytics.google.com
2. Get tracking ID
3. Add tracking code to your site

### 4. Build Quality Backlinks

- List your business on Google My Business
- Get listed in industry directories
- Partner websites linking to you
- Create valuable content that others want to link to

### 5. Update Sitemap Regularly

Whenever you add new pages, update `public/sitemap.xml` with:
- New URL
- Last modified date
- Change frequency
- Priority
